A consice list description of each of my best friends that I've made during my time at McHenry County College.

Friend's Name Description

Isabella Aguilar

Featured with Addie in the left-most image at the top of the page.

One of the first friends I made in college was Isa. We met in an Acting I class two years ago and got to work together on Heathers: The Musical that same semester. Working on the play and being in class together made us a great pair. I loved spending time with Isa because we could share the excitement of both rehearsals and acting exercises. We had a lot of fun in that first semester, and it’s awesome to have stayed friends.

Emma Himley

Featured with Addie in the center image at the top of the page.

Emma is another friend from that Acting I class. We were assigned a scene together, and it turned out to be one of the most challenging scenes I’ve ever done. Working on it together brought us closer, and we both learned a lot. Emma has a cat named Willow, and she’s a huge Taylor Swift fan, which is fun because she always has new Swiftie updates to share.

Skye Schoen

Featured with Addie in the right-most image at the top of the page.

Skye is an awesome friend I met while working on Juliet and Romeo. He did tech work for Heathers, but we didn’t really get to know each other until we acted together the following semester. Skye has this cool, laid-back personality, and we started hanging out as a group with Isa and Emma. He’s a chill and supportive friend, and it’s always a blast hanging out with him.

Nathan Bodecker

Featured with Addie in the left-most image at the bottom of the page.

Nathan is my boyfriend now, but we actually first became close friends during Juliet and Romeo, where he played Romeo and I played Countess Paris. It’s funny to look back on because he had a huge crush on me the whole time. Nathan is a goofball and makes everything more fun. He’s my boyfriend, but also my best friend, which is pretty special.

Olivia Greenwald

Featured with Addie in the center image at the bottom of the page.

Olivia, or Liv, is the friend I can tell absolutely anything to. We have this unique bond where no topic is off-limits, and we don’t hold back any details. Sometimes we grab lunch just to update each other on life, and those conversations are always a highlight. Having a friend like Liv is amazing because everyone needs someone they can be totally open with.
