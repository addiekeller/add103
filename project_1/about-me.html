About Me

Outside of school, I work in retail and enjoy creative activities like music, writing songs, dancing, and acting. I have a family of five, including two brothers. My boyfriend and I met while doing a play at the MCC BlackBox theatre. I love staying busy and using my creativity in different ways, both at school and in my free time.
