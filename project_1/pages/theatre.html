Theatre

My journey in theatre has been an incredible experience filled with creativity and friendships. One of my favorite moments was during my senior show in high school, where I played four characters in The Little Mermaid. My most prominent role was Ursula, the sea witch. I will never forget the standing ovation we received on our closing night. The theatre was packed, and the emotions were overwhelming—it felt amazing to share that moment with my cast and audience.

Since then, I’ve continued to pursue my passion at the MCC BlackBox Theatre. So far, I’ve been in four shows at MCC: Heathers: The Musical, Juliet and Romeo (a gender-swapped version), Frankenstein, and Present Laughter. Each production has been a fun adventure, allowing me to explore different characters and stories. I have met so many wonderful friends and members of the theatre community along the way. Theatre has not only helped me grow as a performer but also connected me with people who share the same passion. I can’t wait to see where my theatre journey takes me next!
