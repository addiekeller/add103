About Us

At Corporation Inc., we believe in making shopping easy, convenient, and fun. Drive-by Pickup

With our drive-by pickup, you can shop online and pick up your items without ever leaving your car. It's fast, easy, and designed for your busy life. Order now from any of our stores and choose a pickup time that works for you.

Why Choose Us?

  • Convenience: Shop online and pick up at a time that works for you, saving time and effort.
  • Variety: We carry groceries, clothes, and auto parts for all your needs.

Our Commitment to the Environment

We’re proud to take steps toward reducing our environmental impact. We offer eco-friendly products and have a recycling policy. Unsold items that aren’t purchased after clearance are donated to local charities to reduce waste.

Giving Back to the Community

Corporation Inc. works closely with local charities in McHenry County to donate unsold clothing items. By shopping with us, you’re helping to give back to the community while getting great products.
