My Art Journey

I’ve always been interested in different kinds of art, and I like trying new things! Sewing is one of my earliest hobbies. I started out with hand-sewing when I was little, and in 2020, I learned how to use a sewing machine. Since then, I've enjoyed making projects like upcycled clothing pieces and other fun designs.
I also love painting. Sometimes, I paint on my bedroom walls, but I also work on canvases and random objects. It’s a relaxing way to get creative, and I usually have music or a podcast playing while I paint.
Recently, I’ve started beading to make necklaces and bracelets. I mostly make them for my boyfriend, using cross-stitch thread and glass beads. This hobby started when I went to the Renaissance faire for the first time last year, and I’ve loved it ever since.
Another favorite is digital art. I’ve been into it since I took a digital imaging class in high school, and now I'm studying graphic design. I use Photoshop a lot and hope to turn this passion into a career.
Another medium I've dabbled in is sculpting. While I don’t sculpt much anymore, I did enjoy making clay projects in my high school 3D-Art class.
Lastly, I love writing music. I play guitar and ukulele and use Bandlab to record my songs. Music has been a big part of my creative journey.
Each of these arts gives me a different way to express myself, and I can’t wait to keep exploring them all!
